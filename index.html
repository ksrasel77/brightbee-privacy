BrightBee is committed to protecting the privacy of its users, especially children. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and disclose information through our mobile application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal information such as name, address, phone number, or location.
  • We do not allow in-app user registration or social media integration.
  • We do not request or access contact lists, photos, or microphone/camera features.

2. AdMob and Third-Party Services

BrightBee uses Google AdMob (Designed for Families) to serve ads. AdMob may collect anonymous usage data, such as:

  • Device type and OS version
  • App usage statistics
  • General location (country-level, not precise)

We have enabled child-directed treatment and tag_for_under_age_of_consent flags as required by Google’s Families policy to ensure safe ads for children.

3. In-App Purchases & External Links

  • We do not offer in-app purchases.
  • All external links (such as “Rate Us”, “Share”, or “Privacy Policy”) are protected behind a parental gate using simple math questions.

4. Children’s Privacy

BrightBee is designed for children under 13. We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play Families policy. We do not knowingly collect or use any personal data from children.

5. Data Security

No data is collected or stored. Any ad-related data collected by AdMob is handled securely and in accordance with their privacy practices.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. You should review this policy periodically.
