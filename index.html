Privacy Policy for BrightBee

Effective Date: May 20, 2025

BrightBee - English and Math for Kids is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information within our mobile application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users. Specifically:

  • No name, email, phone number, or address is collected.
  • No account creation or sign-in is required.
  • We do not access your device’s contacts, photos, camera, or microphone.

2. AdMob and Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob (with child-appropriate settings enabled) to serve ads in the app. Google AdMob may collect limited and anonymized data such as:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage statistics (e.g., screen views, session time)
  • Non-identifiable general location (e.g., country)

We have enabled the tag_for_child_directed_treatment and tag_for_under_age_of_consent flags as required by Google’s Families Policy to ensure child-appropriate ad content.

3. In-App Purchases & External Links

BrightBee does not offer any in-app purchases. Any external links, such as:

  • Rate Us
  • Share This App
  • Privacy Policy

are protected behind a parental gate using a simple math question to prevent accidental access by children.

4. Children’s Privacy

BrightBee is designed for children under 13. We comply with COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act) and Google Play’s Families Policy. We do not knowingly collect, use, or share any personal information from children.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store user data, there is no risk of personal information being exposed. Ad-related data is managed securely by Google AdMob under their privacy and security protocols.

6. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may occasionally update this policy. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and the “Effective Date” will be updated accordingly.
