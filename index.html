Privacy Policy for BrightBee

Effective Date: May 2025

This privacy policy explains how the BrightBee app collects, uses, and protects user information. We are committed to ensuring your privacy and safety, especially for young users.

1. Information We Collect

  • AdMob: We use Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may collect non-personal data such as device information and usage data to improve ad relevance. No personal information is collected by us.
  • Firebase: We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage. It collects anonymized data such as session time, screens visited, and basic device info.
  • We do not collect names, addresses, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information from children or any users.

2. How We Use This Information

  • To serve ads and support app development (via AdMob)
  • To analyze and improve the app’s performance and features (via Firebase)

3. Children’s Privacy

This app is designed for children and complies with COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act). We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal data from children under 13.
